Measuring Weight/Mass Volume

Volume is the amount of space an object contains or occupies. The volume of a container is considered to be the capacity of the container. This is measured by the number of cubic units or the amount of fluid it can contain and not the amount of space the container occupies. The base SI unit for volume is the cubic meter (m3). Aside from cubic meter, another commonly used metric unit for volume of solids is the cubic centimeter (cm3 or cc) while the commonly used metric units for volume of fluids are the liter (L) and the milliliter (mL).
